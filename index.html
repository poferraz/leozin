ABOUT

Based in Vancouver, Canada, I’m a visual storyteller who believes that every frame should capture something authentic—whether it’s the raw beauty of nature, the energy of urban life, or the quiet moments that often go unnoticed.

My Philosophy

Great photography starts with observation and patience. I approach each project with the understanding that the best images emerge when technique meets intuition, when preparation meets spontaneity. My work spans from the intimate textures of analog film to the crisp precision of digital capture, always seeking that moment where light, emotion, and story converge.

Areas of Focus

Nature & Landscape

From Brazil's diverse ecosystems to Canada's vast wilderness, I capture landscapes that showcase the natural world's raw beauty and delicate balance.

Urban Photography

Documenting the pulse of city life, architectural details, and street scenes that tell the evolving stories of modern culture.

Analog & Film

Working with traditional film techniques to create authentic images with the unique character and organic depth that only analog photography provides.

Black & White

Creating timeless monochrome imagery that strips away distractions to reveal pure emotion, contrast, and compositional essence.

Night Photography

Exploring darkness and the interplay between artificial light and shadows, transforming familiar spaces into something mysterious and magical.

Filmmaking

Crafting visual narratives through motion, combining cinematographic techniques with compelling storytelling for documentaries and creative projects.

Creative Process

Each project begins with understanding—understanding the subject, the environment, the story waiting to be told. I work collaboratively, whether documenting a festival’s energy, capturing a brand’s essence, or creating art that explores the intersection between humanity and nature. My technical expertise serves the story, never overshadows it, ensuring each image resonates with authenticity and purpose.
